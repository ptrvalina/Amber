How Amber works

Amber parses a case, builds deterministic profile and anomaly evidence, runs policy checks, then lets the AI layer only narrate already-supported findings.

How anomaly score is built

Scores come from deterministic evidence contributions: thresholds, baselines, tx references, and typology-specific rules. No LLM can redefine the score.

What confidence means

Confidence is a calibration score. It is reduced by weak history, malformed input, low evidence agreement, degraded mode, and emergency mode.

What emergency mode means

Emergency mode keeps the service available when LLM or budget constraints fail. Deterministic evidence still runs; narrative becomes safe fallback text.
